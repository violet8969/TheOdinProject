Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Vanilla
  • Baking powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients in a bowl
  2. Spread batter into a prepared brownie pan
  3. Bake in preheated oven
  4. Cool brownies
  5. Cut into squares or favorite shape
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
